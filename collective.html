What's Inside

The Period Pillars Course

Inside the app, Nicole's Period Pillars course covers the six core areas that influence your cycle — with clear, actionable guidance for each.

Pillar 01

Get to Know Your Flow

Understand your hormonal patterns and learn how to track your cycle effectively using body literacy tools.

Pillar 02

Feed Your Hormones

Nutrition and blood sugar balance — the foods that support your hormones and how to build a plate that works for you.

Pillar 03

Step Off the Blood Sugar Roller Coaster

Understand how blood sugar instability affects your cycle and learn practical strategies for lasting balance.

Pillar 04

Stress Hacks for Modern Life

Stress and nervous system regulation — tools and techniques that actually work for managing chronic overstimulation.

Pillar 05

Fix Your Gut, Fix Your Hormones

Gut health, digestion, and their direct relationship with hormonal balance — and how to address the root causes.

Pillar 06

Love Up Your Liver

Liver function, detoxification, and reducing toxic load — practical steps for supporting hormonal clearance.
