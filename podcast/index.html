About the Show

Periods, Hormones & the Experts Who Get It

The Period Party is a top-rated podcast hosted by Nicole Jardim — Certified Women's Health Coach and author of Fix Your Period. Each week, Nicole sits down with leading practitioners, researchers, and specialists from functional medicine, naturopathic medicine, nutrition, and beyond.

The conversations are honest, science-backed, and refreshingly direct. Expect deep dives into menstrual cycle health, hormonal imbalances, fertility, gut health, thyroid function, perimenopause, and the systems most conventional medicine overlooks. The goal is always the same: give you real information so you can make informed decisions about your own body.

Whether you're brand new to cycle health or years into your healing journey, there's an episode here for you.
